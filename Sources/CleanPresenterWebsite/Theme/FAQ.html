Frequently asked questions

Can’t find the answer you’re looking for?
Shoot us an e-mail at support@cleanpresenter.com.

Is CleanPresenter privacy focussed?
Yes, all features are designed with privacy in mind and happen on your computer. Nothing presented is ever recorded or send of your computer.
Can people join my presentation in Zoom/Meet/Teams/WebEx?
Yes, you can use the screen share feature in Zoom/Meet/Teams/WebEx to share the display showing the presentation. Presenting without an external secondary screen is on our roadmap.
Can I cancel at any time?
Yes, you can cancel at any time. You won't be charged again after cancelling and you keep access to your plan until the end of the current billing period.
Is there a Windows version?
At this moment CleanPresenter is Mac only and heavily optimized for the Apple ecosystem. We don't have plans for a Windows version yet.
